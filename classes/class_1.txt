Class: Sauce

Attributes:

name (string)
isSavory (boolean)
inventory (integer)
dishesThatUse (integer)

Methods:

addSugar (changes the isSavory attribute, also the dishesThatUse attribute)
useInDish (subtracts 1 from current inventory)
modifyRecipe (changes the name attribute to a new string, and the dishesThatUse attribute decreases by 1)
remake (changes the inventory attribute)
